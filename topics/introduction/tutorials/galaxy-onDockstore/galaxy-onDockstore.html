Dockstore follows the FAIR Guiding Principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable and Reusable). ## Findable - Users are strongly suggested to include robust metadata and human readable instructions. Dockstore parses provided metadata and allows it to be searchable. - The faceted search feature allows users to find relevant workflows using search terms and filter their search using workflow language, author, organization, labels, etc. - Users can explore workflows from the Organizations and Collections pages where institutions and organizations can showcase their workflows and tools by grouping them by their research theme.
